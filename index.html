Cave Adventure Go

Escape from the lava! In the game, you will be a pilot to escape from the lava that chasing you behind. You need to click the screen in order to keep the flight floating. Avoid touching any rock in cave since they will hinder the flight moving forward.

飞行员在探索地穴中遭到岩浆的攻击！在游戏中，你会扮演飞行员逃离岩浆的攻击。你需要点击屏幕保持飞行器向上移动，同时躲避岩石，因为它们会让你减慢速度。
